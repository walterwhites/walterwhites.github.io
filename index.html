Wikiday

Android app

I propose you an application completely free and without ads. It allows to load an infinity of article from a category that you choose.

features:

  • Get an article about a chosen category (History for example)
  • Get a random article (on all subjects)
  • Get an article with an expression or phrase you have chosen
  • Add favorite categories and articles
  • Subscribe to categories to receive notifications (soon)

50 categories available:

  • history
  • geography
  • science
  • computer
  • art
  • religion
  • particle physics
  • and still full of others.
