Ethics and Responsibility

Fairness and Bias

Fairness and bias in machine learning refer to the ethical considerations related to the potential discrimination or unfairness that can arise from algorithms and models. Ensuring fairness is crucial in responsible AI development.

Purpose

The purpose of understanding Fairness and Bias in machine learning is to address and mitigate the biases that can emerge in AI systems, promoting ethical and equitable AI applications.
